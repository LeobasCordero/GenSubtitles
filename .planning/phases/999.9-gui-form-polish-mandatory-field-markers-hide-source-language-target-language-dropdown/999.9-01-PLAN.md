<objective>
Apply three bundled form micro-improvements to the CustomTkinter GUI (gensubtitles/gui/main.py):

1. Append ` *:` to the two mandatory field labels so required inputs are visually flagged.
2. Hide the source-language row entirely — Whisper auto-detection makes it unnecessary noise.
3. Replace the target-language free-text entry with a `CTkOptionMenu` dropdown
   (Spanish / English / Other); selecting "Other" reveals a secondary free-text entry.

Purpose: Improves UX clarity and reduces user error. No backend/API changes needed.
Output: Updated gensubtitles/gui/main.py committed on a feature branch.
</objective>

def _on_target_lang_change(self, selection: str) -> None:
    """Show/hide the 'Other' free-text entry based on dropdown selection."""

        self._entry_target_lang_other.grid(
            row=4, column=1, columnspan=2, sticky="ew", pady=4

    else:
        self._entry_target_lang_other.grid_remove()
